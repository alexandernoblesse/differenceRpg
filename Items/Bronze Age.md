# Ancient / Bronze Age Equipment

### Economy: The Ring Standard
In this era, standardized coinage is not in use. Wealth is measured in **Rings** of bronze, silver, and gold, representing a set weight of metal used in trade and barter.

* **Currency:** Bronze Ring (BR), Silver Ring (SR), Gold Ring (GR)
* **Exchange Rate:** 10 Bronze Rings = 1 Silver Ring; 10 Silver Rings = 1 Gold Ring.

> **Healer's Kit (Bronze Age):** Contains linen bandages, a bronze needle and thread, wooden splints, and basic herbal salves. A character must have this kit to attempt `Medicine` skill checks to treat **Minor** and **Moderate Injuries**. Using the kit grants a **Minor Edge (+1)** to these checks. The kit has 5 uses before it must be restocked.
