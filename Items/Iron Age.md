# Iron / Medieval Era Equipment

### Economy: Standard Coinage
This era uses a standard system of minted coins, typically controlled by kingdoms or powerful city-states.

* **Currency:** Gold Piece (gp), Silver Piece (sp), Copper Piece (cp)
* **Exchange Rate:** 10 Copper Pieces = 1 Silver Piece; 10 Silver Pieces = 1 Gold Piece.

> **Healer's Kit:** Contains bandages, a needle and thread, splints, and a basic herbal salve. A character must have this kit to attempt `Medicine` skill checks to treat **Minor** and **Moderate Injuries**. Using a Healer's Kit grants a **Minor Edge (+1)** to these checks. The kit has 5 uses before it must be restocked.
> **Surgeon's Tools:** A set of specialized knives, saws, and probes. Required to attempt `Medicine` skill checks to treat **Severe Injuries**, such as performing surgery.

> Items marked with an asterisk (*) are for roleplaying purposes and don’t have specific statistics or rules.
